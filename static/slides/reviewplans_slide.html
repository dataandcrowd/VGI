Temporary Title



Factors Negatively affecting the Participation of Geographic Crowdsourcing Projects: a Systematic Review

Motivation

  • Why do we see biases in participatory mapping projects?

  • If so, what are the main factors associated with the bias?

Motivation for a Systematic Review

  • Several papers have investigated possible factors that hinder the participation in crowdsourced mapping in their VGI projects

  • Most empirical studies have extensive questions which can be less generalisable

  • To our knowledge, none of the published reviews identified or analysed the available evidence from a strict systematic approach

Advantages of a Systematic Review

  • To improve decision that are maximally informed and minimally biased
  • The transparency of the selection criteria is a safe bet with academic markers and journal peer reviewers ⇒ “how and why were the studies included or excluded?”
  • Far less stressful and far more manageable

Research Objective and Questions

Objective

  • To review and analyse the main factors that underlie a major barrier for Public VGI projects

Research Questions

  • What factors are associated with the under-represent of participation in VGI projects?
  • Are there other non-demographic factors that can hinder VGI projects which are not revealed yet?
